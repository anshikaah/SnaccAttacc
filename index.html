About Us


A World of Flavours at Your Doorstep

Welcome to SnaccAttacc, your one-stop shop for indulging in a global culinary adventure, all from the comfort of your home! We were founded by Anshika Sinha, a passionate foodie who dreamt of creating a seamless way to explore the diverse world of cuisine without the hassle of visiting multiple restaurants.

Anshika envisioned a platform that would connect food lovers with a variety of restaurants and cuisines, offering a delightful and convenient dining experience. We believe that food has the power to bring people together, and we're here to make that experience effortless and delightful. So, explore our diverse menu, discover new favourites, and embark on a culinary journey with every bite!
